NG-NetMS

NG-NetMS is a new end-to-end network management platform for your Linux servers, Cisco, Juniper, HP and Extreme routers, switches and firewalls.

    NG-NetMS was developed by network engineers for network engineers!

    We successfully used NG-NetMS for delivery of network assessment services worldwide. We could deliver our projects quickly, for a fraction of the cost of the similar services from leading equipment vendors.

    We can do this faster and better with NG-NetMS, and we hope that you will do too!

    We want to give you full visibility to the chaotic world of your network infrastructure.

    • See your network as it really is
    • Uncover hidden or lost assets
    • See what's going on in your network to the lowest possible level of details
    • Calm your "network seas" (you will know what this means after you try NG-NetMS) ;-)
